Bolognese

A beautiful Bolognese pasta

Pasta Bolognese is a classic Italian dish where al dente pasta meets a hearty ground beef sauce slow-cooked with onions, garlic, and tomatoes.

The addition of milk helps tenderize the beef and also adds a note of richness to the flavorful bolognese sauce. Itâ€™s a family-friendly dinner everyone will love!

Ingredients

  • Lean Ground geef
  • Tomatoes
  • Tagliatelle pasta

Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large heavy-bottomed pot.
  2. Add the beef and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Add crusheddd tomatoes,bay leaf, oregano and nutmeg.
  4. Bring a large pot of water to boil. Serve the pasta and spoon the Bolognese over it.
